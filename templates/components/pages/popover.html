Usage

<div x-data="apexPopover()" class="relative inline-block">
  <button @click="toggle()">Open</button>
  <div x-show="open" x-cloak @click.outside="close()"
       class="absolute top-full mt-1 …">
    {# your content #}
  </div>
</div>

Accessibility

  • • Trigger uses aria-expanded + aria-haspopup.
  • • Popover has role="menu" (action menus) or role="dialog" (info / forms).
  • • Menu items use role="menuitem".
  • • Click outside and Esc both close.
