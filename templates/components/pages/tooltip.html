Accessibility

  • • Tooltip element has role="tooltip".
  • • Trigger uses aria-describedby to associate the tooltip when used via the include.
  • • Tooltip shows on both mouseenter and focus — keyboard-only users see it too.
  • • pointer-events-none on the tooltip prevents it from blocking the trigger.
  • • Animations gated on prefers-reduced-motion.
